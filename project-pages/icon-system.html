Visual Type

Icon system for Setu

At Setu, I contributed to defining a visual style, establishing guidelines for icons, and creating approximately 150 of them.

These pictograms proved to be incredibly versatile, finding their way into the website, sales decks, print collaterals like employee ID cards, and even Slack emojis representing company values.


Special thanks to Prajna for defining the style and Sujan for his guidance throughout the process.

Purpose and Scope

At Setu, the team always ensures that every piece we create together is crafted with care and attention to detail. We believe in subtle, stoic design.

USE CASES

We started by focusing on using the icons for the website and pitch decks, as Setu was in its early stages, transitioning from zero to one.


AUDIENCE

The idea was to initially use these icons within the design team and gradually make them accessible to all other teams.
